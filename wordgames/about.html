About this site

A small notebook of word games

Inkwell Puzzles is a free collection of word search, scramble, and trivia games, built to be quick to play on a phone with no account and no downloads.

This site is run independently and updated with new puzzles and articles over time. Every game runs directly in your browser — no personal data is required to play.

Have a puzzle idea, spot a bug, or want to suggest a word list? Get in touch on the contact page.
